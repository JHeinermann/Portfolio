Covid was here!

How Covid spread throughout Germany

In winter 2020, Germany went into a Lockdown Light. I was sitting at home, curious about the spatio-temporal distribution of Covid-cases. But even on the internet, I did not find what I was looking for. I wanted a map with all districts (Landkreise) to be plotted over time so you could see the spatial distribution of Covid-cases during the pandemic. At that time, detailed Covid data was publicly available (I wanted to make an update of the video but I did not find the data anymore. Please contact me, if you know where to find this data) and thus I decided to create my own map. I was inspired by the dashboard of the RKI but also by the youtube channel Data Is Beautiful. On the channel, you can find great animated bar charts that are perfect for visualizing the development of events. I also wanted to highlight the age structure of people with a Covid infection. I thought this might fit in one video, and here it is.
